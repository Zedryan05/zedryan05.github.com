About the Website:

Compass is an instrument used for navigation that shows direction, it points you to your destination. Just like a compass, this website aims to direct people towards the right path. It leads people to be more aware about the ongoing social issues in our society. It points the location to different social issues in society so that we could make it a better place. Finally, it will navigate you to a destination wherein unity and teamwork among people exists. These are the aims of this website.
